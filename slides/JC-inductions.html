Journal Club Inductions

General Instructions

  1. Your submitted work is not expected to be a piece of art. Just stay in your comfort zone and make an entry; we praise enthusiasm more than perfection among freshers.
  2. Plagiarism will not be entertained.

Task 1

  • Write 400-500 words about any of the given topics.
  • There are 9 topics in total, and they cover a number of categories like sports, politics and technology.

Click the down arrow to view the topics.

(1) Queen Elizabeth II, the longest reigning female monarch, died in September 2022. For Commonwealth countries, now the last link to the empire is gone. Will Charles III be a welcome head of state? How do you think her demise will impact the political future of the UK?

(2) The recently concluded Asia cup tournament served as a huge disappointment for the Indian cricket fans. Write a comprehensive piece, analyzing the possible reasons/mistakes owing up to the failure and whether this puts India's T20 world cup dreams in jeopardy.

(3) Recent international research has revealed that Pegasus Spyware was used as an electronic tool of surveillance against at least 300 individuals in India. The number includes opposition leaders, civil society activists, journalists, and even a sitting Supreme Court Judge. Are we falling prey to no personal data privacy in the name of security? Give your views on the growing breach of right to privacy in recent times, with the government emphasizing on getting their hands over as much public information as possible.

(4) India will assume the presidency of the G20 from December and host the summit of leaders of the world’s twenty largest economies in New Delhi. Write explaining how this will affect India and the world amidst the global recession and the global tensions.

(5) Mikhail Gorbachev passed away on 30th August, 2022. UNSG Antonio Guterres said, “He changed the course of history”. He served as the last president of the USSR and introduced several reforms before it voted to end itself. Write on what is left of his legacy, his contributions to the Soviet Union and how Russians still seem to be against his ideologies.

(6) The global pandemic has forced millions globally to work-from-home (WFH). Industries in India such as construction, manufacturing, and hospitality faced a severe hit, whereas major tech giants were seen continuing WFH even deep into 2022. Write about how the different industries managed to pull through with WFH and the business developments it brought about.

(7) The Artemis mission is guzzling up huge amounts of money,and has been delayed for over 6 years, exhausting over $93 billion. Is this white elephant worth the trouble or could the funds have been pumped into something more productive rather than just a mad race for space influence?

Task 2

Write a short report evaluating, criticizing or commenting (sarcastically) on any event, occasion, problem, nuisance or reality of campus or inventing an imaginary event on campus that relates to a what-if situation. (200-250 words)

Towards the end, write a paragraph on why you wish to join the Journal Club and what you expect from the club and as a part of the student community, what would you like to read about in our articles?

Examples: dogs on campus, bon-voyage, hostel in-time, covid protocols, mess food, mess registration, ERP registration, hostel washrooms, interactions, mettl platform, SAC, etc.
